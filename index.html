ngHandsontable is a wrapper for Handsontable library

This is ngHandsontable , a release published on Sep 19, 2014.

hot-table is main and required directive for ngHandsontable
You can put all your settings in settings = "{colHeaders: colHeaders, contextMenu: true }" attribute
or in separated attributes like minSpareRows = "minSpareRows", rowHeaders = "false"
